Getting Started for AirNode Operators

1

Create an Account

Sign up to purchase AirNodes and access the coverage map. Create an account

2

Choose Your AirNode

Select the AirNode model that suits your community’s needs, existing coverage, and investment capacity.

3

Decide on Hosting

Operate and host the AirNode yourself as a Host Operator, or own it remotely with a Host managing deployment as a Remote Operator. Cellular models (Portal and Titan) require a Host, while fixed wireless models (Link and Apex) can be self-hosted.

4

Select a Location

Pick an available hex from the interactive map.

5

Host Terms Agreement

If using a Host, discuss and finalize terms before completing your purchase.

6

Deployment & Activation

Portal & Titan AirNodes require deployment at the Host location. Link & Apex AirNodes can be self-setup or are pre-deployed.

7

Manage Your AirNode

Track AirNode performance, connectivity data, and operational statistics through your dashboard.

8

Earn Rewards

View your rewards in the dashboard within 48 hours of deployment. Customize your payment preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EarthNode?
EarthNodes are the backbone of the World Mobile network, responsible for processing transactions, executing smart contracts, and securing the blockchain. They enable the decentralized sharing economy and reward operators with $WMTx tokens for their contributions.
What is an ENNFT?
ENNFT stands for EarthNode Non-Fungible Token. It represents ownership of an EarthNode and grants operators the right to participate in World Mobile's decentralized ecosystem. Only 1,000 ENNFTs exist, and they are tokenized on the Cardano blockchain.
How do I qualify to operate an EarthNode?
To operate an EarthNode, you need to own an ENNFT. Additionally, you'll need reliable technical infrastructure, including a stable internet connection and computing resources, to ensure efficient node performance.

How are EarthNode operators rewarded?
EarthNode operators earn $WMTx through block production, transaction validation, and additional services like storage-as-a-service. Rewards are based on network activity and the amount of $WMTx delegated to their EarthNode.
Can I sell or transfer my EarthNode?
Yes, ENNFTs can be sold or transferred on digital marketplaces. Ownership is recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and security. The ENNFT always retains a minimum value equivalent to 100,000 $WMTx.
What role do EarthNodes play in World Mobile's ecosystem?
EarthNodes validate transactions, manage decentralized identities, and ensure data integrity. They are integral to the World Mobile Chain, connecting AirNodes and other components of the decentralized network.
What support is available for EarthNode operators?
World Mobile Club offers detailed technical documentation and an active community forums to assist operators in setting up and maintaining their EarthNodes efficiently.
