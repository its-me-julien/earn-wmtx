How WMTx Powers the World Mobile Network

Discover the innovative sharing economy driving decentralized mobile connectivity. Here's how WMTx fuels the World Mobile ecosystem:

  • 1 - Network Revenue Generation

    Customers pay for calls, texts, and data in local currencies, creating revenue for the network.

  • 2 - EarthNode Rewards

    EarthNode operators and stakers earn WMTx by processing transactions and delivering services for network customers.

  • 3 - Market Repurchase

    World Mobile uses network revenue to purchase WMTx from open markets, ensuring decentralized allocation.

  • 4 - Operator & Staker Rewards

    Operators and stakers are rewarded with WMTx purchased from open markets.

  • 5 - Fixed Supply Advantage

    With a fixed supply, WMTx ensures scalability and long-term value, even as the network expands.

Power the blockchain-based sharing economy of World Mobile. Whether you're bringing connectivity as an AirNode Operator, processing transactions with an EarthNode, staking WMTx, or contributing through the World Mobile app, there's a rewarding role for everyone.
