Scan for Points

Join the World Mobile network revolution by scanning for points. Help identify network gaps, suggest new AirNode locations, and earn rewards while enhancing global connectivity.

About Scan for Points

What is Scan for Points?

Scan for Points is a gamified tool that lets World Mobile users help expand the network by scanning and collecting valuable data on connectivity performance.

Who Can Use Scan for Points?

Anyone with the World Mobile Android app can scan while walking, running, or traveling, contributing to network improvements.

Why Use Scan for Points?

Earn loyalty points for enhancing connectivity and enjoy benefits like value-added services while supporting global network growth.

Get the World Mobile App

How to Begin Scanning for Points

Start contributing to the World Mobile network and earn points effortlessly by scanning your surroundings. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Step 1: Download the World Mobile app from the Google Play Store.
  • Step 2: Open the app and start scanning to contribute valuable network data.
  • Step 3: Earn points redeemable for network benefits and services.

How to Read the Scan for Points Map

How to Read the Scan for Points Map

Use the Scan for Points map to monitor your contributions to the World Mobile network and identify earning opportunities. Here's how to interpret the hex colors:

  • Yellow Hex: Represents a fully scanned area with a checkmark. All data has been collected.
  • Grey Hex: Indicates that all points in the hex have been claimed by other users.
  • Green Hex: Shows an active opportunity to earn points, ranging from 1 to 5, depending on previous scans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scan for Points?

Scan for Points is a gamified feature that incentivizes World Mobile users to contribute to the network's development. By scanning with your phone, you help identify areas with weak coverage and potential AirNode locations.

How does Scan for Points work?

Using the World Mobile Android app, your phone connects to the nearest network to collect data such as ping, download, and upload speeds. This data ensures the network expands where connectivity is most needed.

Who can participate in Scan for Points?

Anyone with an Android device can participate in Scan for Points. Scanning helps improve the network while earning rewards.

What rewards can I earn?

Participants earn loyalty points that can be redeemed for network benefits, discounts or value-added services like VPN features and DID services.

Where can I scan?

You can scan anywhere in the world. Your contributions help map the network globally and improve connectivity in underserved regions.

How do I read the map?

Fully scanned hexes are shaded yellow and display a check mark. Grey hexes mean all points have been claimed, while green hexes indicate available points to earn.

How often should I scan?

Frequent scanning provides more valuable data. Walking is the most effective method for thorough scans, as it ensures you capture all hexes in your area.

Can I scan while driving or using public transportation?

Yes, but walking ensures more accurate scans as you’re less likely to miss hexes due to speed. Driving and other fast travel methods may result in gaps in your scans.

How long does it take to receive rewards?

Points are typically awarded within 48 hours of scanning.

Is the Scan for Points app available on iOS?

Currently, the app is only available for Android devices. Stay tuned for updates on iOS compatibility.
