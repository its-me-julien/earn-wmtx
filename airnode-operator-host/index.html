Host & Operate an AirNode

Operate and host an AirNode from your own property to start earning rewards while providing reliable internet connectivity to your local community.

What is an AirNode?

An AirNode is a wireless access point that connects local users to World Mobile's decentralized network, providing reliable internet, voice calls, and text messaging services in areas where traditional networks fail to reach.

What is a Host Operator?

A Host Operator combines the roles of hosting and operating an AirNode. By providing a suitable location and managing operations, Host Operators help expand connectivity while earning rewards for their contributions.

What is required?

Becoming a Host Operator requires minimal setup. Here's what you need:

  • A suitable property for installing the AirNode
  • Reliable power supply to keep the AirNode operational
  • Ability to monitor and maintain the equipment if needed

Frequently Asked Questions

What Are the Requirements to Become an AirNode Host Operator?

To become an AirNode Host Operator, you need access to a suitable property location for AirNode installation, a reliable power supply, and a stable business-grade internet connection. Additional requirements may include providing occasional maintenance access.

How Does the Host Operator Role Work?

Host Operators combine hosting and operational responsibilities. This means you will not only provide the location and support for the AirNode but also oversee its functionality and connectivity to ensure it serves your community effectively.

What Are the Financial Benefits of Becoming a Host Operator?

Host Operators earn rewards based on the connectivity services provided by their AirNode. Earnings increase as more users connect through your AirNode, creating a sustainable income stream.

What Technical Support Is Available for Host Operators?

World Mobile offers comprehensive support for setup and maintenance. Detailed guides, video tutorials, and a dedicated support team ensure that even operators with no technical experience can manage their AirNodes effectively.

Does Hosting an AirNode Impact My Internet Performance?

The impact on your internet performance is minimal. The AirNode uses a small portion of your bandwidth, and with a strong business-grade connection, you should not notice significant slowdowns.

How Secure Is the Data Used by AirNodes?

World Mobile prioritizes user privacy and data security. Traffic volume metrics visible on the dashboard are anonymized, and all user data is encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.

Can I Host Multiple AirNodes on My Property?

Yes, hosting multiple AirNodes is possible if your location and resources meet the requirements. This can enhance your earning potential and network impact.

What Happens If My AirNode Requires Repairs?

In the rare event of a malfunction, World Mobile provides repair or replacement support. Hosts are required to report any issues promptly to minimize service interruptions.

How Do I Get Started as a Host Operator?

Getting started involves creating an account, selecting your AirNode type, confirming your location, and finalizing the purchase. World Mobile will assist with deployment and activation.
