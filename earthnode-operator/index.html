Getting Started for AirNode Operators

1

Create an Account

Sign up to purchase AirNodes and access the coverage map. Create an account

2

Choose Your AirNode

Select the AirNode model that suits your community’s needs, existing coverage, and investment capacity.

3

Decide on Hosting

Operate and host the AirNode yourself as a Host Operator, or own it remotely with a Host managing deployment as a Remote Operator. Cellular models (Portal and Titan) require a Host, while fixed wireless models (Link and Apex) can be self-hosted.

4

Select a Location

Pick an available hex from the interactive map.

5

Host Terms Agreement

If using a Host, discuss and finalize terms before completing your purchase.

6

Deployment & Activation

Portal & Titan AirNodes require deployment at the Host location. Link & Apex AirNodes can be self-setup or are pre-deployed.

7

Manage Your AirNode

Track AirNode performance, connectivity data, and operational statistics through your dashboard.

8

Earn Rewards

View your rewards in the dashboard within 48 hours of deployment. Customize your payment preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I Qualify as an AirNode Operator?

Financial Requirements: You must have the ability to make upfront payments for your chosen AirNode packages. The investment varies by model, ranging from $62.99 for a Spark AirNode to $100,000 for a Titan AirNode.
Technical Experience: No prior technical experience is required to participate in World Mobile's sharing economy. The network provides support and guidance for setup and maintenance.

How Many AirNodes Can I Purchase?

You can buy up to 10 AirNodes in a single transaction on the World Mobile network. However, there is no total limit on AirNode ownership - individuals and organizations can own as many AirNodes as they wish through multiple transactions.

What's Included in AirNode Packages?

Each AirNode package comes complete with all necessary equipment for setup. The first year's hex and AirNode fees are included, along with required licenses and operating software.

What Payment Methods Are Accepted?

World Mobile accepts card payments, Crypto.com, and bank transfers for AirNode purchases. Bank transfers require a non-refundable reservation fee and typically process in 5-7 business days.
