The Problem

The Problem:
3 Billion Unconnected

Nearly half the world’s population lacks consistent internet access. This digital divide hinders education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, perpetuating inequality globally.

Connectivity Challenges in Developed Countries

150 millions people in developed countries remain offline due to cost barriers and limited rural infrastructure.

Connectivity Challenges in Developing Countries

In emerging economies, approximately 40% of residents lack mobile internet connectivit - due to high costs, logistical barriers, and lack of investment in sustainable telecom solutions.

The Limits of Traditional Telecom

High capital expenditures, regulatory hurdles, and reliance on centralized infrastructure make it economically unviable for traditional telecom operators to expand into underserved areas.

What is World Mobile?

What Makes World Mobile Unique?

World Mobile redefines telecom by combining blockchain technology and community participation to deliver affordable, sustainable, and inclusive connectivity.

  • Efficient Connectivity: By leveraging decentralized infrastructure and innovative tech, World Mobile delivers internet access at up to 12x lower costs than legacy telecom providers.
  • Shared Ownership Model: Infrastructure (AirNodes) is owned and operated by individuals and communities, ensuring that value flows back to participants.
  • Resilient Global Network: Built on blockchain, the network is scalable, secure, and immune to single points of failure, making it ideal for both urban centers and underserved areas.

Blockchain Technology

Blockchain: The Backbone of World Mobile

The World Mobile Chain (WMC), powered by the Base blockchain, ensures transparency, security, and scalability.

  • Transparency: WMC provides an immutable, verifiable ledger of transactions, ensuring trust and accountability throughout the network.
  • $WMTX Token: The native utility token powers all on-chain transactions, including data usage fees, staking, and rewards. With a multi-chain presence, $WMTX ensures liquidity and accessibility across ecosystems.
  • EarthNodes: Integral to the network, EarthNodes validate transactions, ensuring that the network operates efficiently.
  • Base Integration: Leveraging Base's scalability and low fees, the World Mobile Chain offers faster transactions, making decentralized telecom viable for millions worldwide.

Understanding Tokenomics

Tokenomics: The Power of $WMTX

$WMTX is the native utility token of the World Mobile network. It drives all transactions, rewards participants, and supports the decentralized telecom ecosystem.

  • Utility: $WMTX is used for on-chain transaction fees, staking, and rewarding AirNode and EarthNode operators.
  • Supply Model: With a fixed maximum supply of 2 billion tokens, $WMTX employs a carefully tapered inflation model to incentivize early adoption and long-term growth.
  • Rewards: Node operators and stakers earn $WMTX based on transaction volume and network activity, ensuring alignment between contributions and rewards.

As network usage grows, demand for $WMTX increases, enhancing its value while maintaining sustainability through its fixed supply and decreasing inflation curve.
