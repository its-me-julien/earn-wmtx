The Problem

The Problem:
3 Billion Unconnected

Nearly half the world’s population lacks consistent internet access. This digital divide hinders education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, perpetuating inequality globally.

Connectivity Challenges in Developed Countries

150 millions people in developed countries remain offline due to cost barriers and limited rural infrastructure.

Connectivity Challenges in Developing Countries

In emerging economies, approximately 40% of residents lack mobile internet connectivit - due to high costs, logistical barriers, and lack of investment in sustainable telecom solutions.

The Limits of Traditional Telecom

High capital expenditures, regulatory hurdles, and reliance on centralized infrastructure make it economically unviable for traditional telecom operators to expand into underserved areas.

What is World Mobile?

What Makes World Mobile Unique?

World Mobile redefines telecom by combining new technology, blockchain and community participation.

  • Efficient Connectivity: By leveraging decentralized infrastructure and innovative tech, World Mobile delivers internet access at up to 12x lower costs than legacy telecom providers.
  • Shared Ownership Model: Infrastructure (AirNodes), planning (Hexes), computing (EarthNodes) are owned and operated by the community, ensuring that value flows back to participants.
  • Resilient Global Network: Built on blockchain, the network is scalable, secure, and immune to single points of failure.

Blockchain Technology

Blockchain: The Backbone of World Mobile

The World Mobile Chain (WMC), powered by Base, ensures transparency, security, and scalability.

  • Transparency: WMC provides an immutable, verifiable ledger of transactions, ensuring trust and accountability.
  • $WMTX Token: The native utility token powers all on-chain transactions. With a multi-chain presence, $WMTX ensures liquidity and accessibility across ecosystems.
  • The Core: EarthNodes validate transactions, ensuring that the network operates efficiently.

How World Mobile Works

World Mobile infrastructure operates through three interconnected components.

EarthNodes

EarthNodes form the network’s backbone, processing transactions and managing core services through distributed servers.

AirNodes

AirNodes are the physical infrastructure that brings connectivity to communities.

Aether Nodes

The Aether Nodes interface with legacy telecommunications networks and handle protocol translations, media transcoding, and routing traffic to these networks.

Understanding Tokenomics

Tokenomics: The World Mobile Economy

$WMTX powers the circular economic incentives of the World Mobile Chain.

  • Utility: $WMTX is used for all on-chain transaction fees.
  • Supply Model: With a fixed maximum supply of 2 billion tokens, $WMTX employs a decelerating inflation model to incentivize early adoption.

As network usage grows, demand for $WMTX increases, enhancing its value while maintaining sustainability through its fixed supply and decreasing inflation curve.
