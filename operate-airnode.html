Operate an AirNode

Join World Mobile's mission to bridge the digital divide. As an AirNode Operator, you can bring reliable internet connectivity to underserved areas while earning rewards in WMTx tokens.

What is an AirNode?

An AirNode is a vital component of World Mobile's decentralized telecommunications network. These devices provide voice, text, and data services, creating reliable connectivity in areas where traditional networks fail to reach.

How Do AirNodes Work?

AirNodes connect users to the internet via a hybrid mesh network. By linking to EarthNodes for authentication and service management, AirNodes ensure secure, private, and efficient connectivity. All activity is transparently recorded on the blockchain.

Who Can Operate an AirNode?

Operating an AirNode is open to everyone, from individuals to businesses. Whether you opt for a small Spark AirNode for residential coverage or a Titan for large-scale solutions, you can tailor your involvement based on your goals and community needs.

Earn Rewards as an AirNode Operator

AirNode Operators earn WMTx tokens by providing internet access. As more users connect, your revenue grows, creating a sustainable and rewarding opportunity.

How to Get Started

1. Choose Your Role

Decide whether to be a remote operator owning AirNodes or a host operator managing both ownership and physical setup for maximum earnings.

2. Manage Your Dashboard

Once active, track your rewards in real time. Customize your payout preferences and monitor user connections via a personalized dashboard.
