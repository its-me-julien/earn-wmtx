Host an AirNode

An AirNode is the core building block of World Mobile's network, bringing internet and cellular connectivity to communities worldwide. As an AirNode Host, you can participate in this revolutionary network simply by providing a suitable location for an AirNode installation.

You don't need to purchase any equipment—World Mobile's AirNode operators handle that. What you provide is access to a powered, connected location where an AirNode can effectively serve your community. In return, you earn rewards as part of World Mobile's sharing economy while helping expand affordable connectivity in your area.

This unique hosting opportunity connects you with experienced network operators who handle the technical aspects, backed by World Mobile's extensive telecommunications expertise.

Requirements

  • A suitable location
  • Reliable power supply for the AirNode
  • Ability to provide maintenance access when needed

Join the Connectivity Revolution

Become part of a global network of innovators transforming the telecommunications industry. Your participation helps bring affordable internet access to underserved communities.

No Purchase or Technical Expertise Required

You don't need to purchase or maintain any equipment. AirNode Operators handle the technical aspects while you provide the essential location and infrastructure support. World Mobile's experienced team ensures smooth operation and provides ongoing guidance.

Earn Passive Rewards

By hosting an AirNode, you receive a share of the rewards generated from providing connectivity to your community. Your earnings grow as more users connect through your hosted AirNode.
