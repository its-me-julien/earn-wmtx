Host an AirNode

Host an AirNode to bring connectivity to your community, earn rewards, and join the World Mobile network revolution—no equipment or technical expertise required.

What is an AirNode?

An AirNode is the core building block of World Mobile's network, bringing internet and cellular connectivity to communities worldwide.

What does an AirNode Host do?

As an AirNode Host, you can participate in this revolutionary network simply by providing a suitable location for an AirNode installation.

What is required?

You don't need to purchase any equipment—World Mobile's AirNode operators handle that. You just need:

  • A suitable location
  • Reliable power supply for the AirNode
  • Ability to provide maintenance access when needed
